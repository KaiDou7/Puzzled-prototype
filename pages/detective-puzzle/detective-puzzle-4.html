4. Climbing Trip

A couple went on for a climbing trip. But only the husband returned from the vacation and said that his wife slipped off while climbing and died. On investigating, the local sheriff arrested him saying, "Your travel agent called. You murdered your wife." The man did not inform anyone about the trip.

Then how did the agent was so sure that it is a murder?

The man bought a two way ticket for his wife and a one way ticket for himself. The man walked. The man bought only a one way ticket for his wife whereas he bought a two way ticket for himself. The man took the train to go, and came back on foot.

Answer:The man bought only a one way ticket for his wife whereas he bought a two way ticket for himself.

This shows that he planned to not come back with his wife which likely makes him the biggest suspect. Some even say that the agent must've seen the husband killing the wife but with the given options, above answer is the best bet
